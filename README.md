# 📊 Inventory Stock Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Inventory Stock Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI**.
The objective of the project is to analyze inventory data, monitor stock health, identify low-stock risks, and visualize product performance across categories.

The dashboard helps simulate how businesses track **inventory levels, product distribution, and stock risk** to support operational decision-making.

---

## 📁 Dataset Information

The dataset used in this project is **synthetic inventory data** created for analytical and visualization purposes.

It simulates a retail inventory system containing information such as:

* Product Name
* Product Category
* Size Variations
* Stock Quantity
* Product Ratings
* Product Price
* Stock Status

The dataset contains **10,000 product records across multiple categories** to demonstrate real-world inventory analysis.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Inventory Overview

The overview page provides a high-level summary of inventory metrics including:

* Total Products
* Total Stock Quantity
* Total Categories
* Average Product Rating
* Average Product Price

Additional visualizations include:

* Inventory Status Distribution
* Stock Quantity by Product Category
* Product Distribution by Category
* Product Size Distribution

---

### 2️⃣ Stock Analysis

This page focuses on identifying **inventory risks and product performance**.

Key visualizations include:

* Products by Risk Category
* Inventory Health Distribution
* Products by Rating Risk Level
* Low Stock Products Table
* Top Products by Inventory Quantity
* Product Ratings by Product

---

## 🔎 Key Insights

* The majority of inventory is categorized as **Healthy Stock**, indicating stable stock levels.
* A smaller portion of products fall under the **Low Stock category**, highlighting potential restocking requirements.
* Electronics and Clothing categories contain the **largest inventory quantities**.
* Certain products such as **Smartphones and Headphones** maintain the highest stock quantities.
* Product ratings show that most items fall into the **Good Rating category**, suggesting positive customer feedback.
